Company Overview

Who We Are

Zeem Global Publications Limited is a leading educational publishing company based in Nigeria, specialising in curriculum-based learning resources for pre-primary, primary, and secondary schools. Since our founding in 2011, we have built a strong reputation for quality, relevance, and reliability in educational publishing.

The company was established with a clear and unwavering purpose: to produce value-based learning materials that guarantee quality assurance in the education sector. That purpose guides everything we do — from selecting subject matter experts to setting the standards for our printing house.

We have established a strong operational presence in the South-western region of Nigeria, Port Harcourt, and Abuja (the Federal Capital Territory), serving thousands of schools and learners across these areas.

Mission Statement

Complete Printing Press Operations
