Educational Publishing Solutions

Built for Every Stage of Learning

Zeem Global Publications offers a comprehensive suite of educational publishing products designed to support learners, educators, and schools throughout Nigeria. Our materials are curriculum-aligned, professionally designed, and produced to the highest standards at our in-house printing facility in Ibadan.

Core Products

Educational Publishing Categories

Our primary product lines serve the full spectrum of Nigeria's pre-primary and primary education system.

🌱

Pre-Nursery Textbooks

Specially designed for children aged 2–3, our Pre-Nursery series lays the earliest foundations of learning. Using vibrant illustrations, large type, and age-appropriate activities, these books introduce colours, shapes, numbers, letters, and basic comprehension in an engaging and child-friendly format.

Ages 2–3 Literacy Foundations Numeracy Foundations Motor Skills
📗

Nursery Textbooks

Our Nursery series is crafted for children aged 3–5, building on early foundations to develop reading readiness, number sense, and creative expression. Colourful and interactive, these materials make learning joyful while meeting the requirements of Nigeria's early childhood education guidelines.

Ages 3–5 Pre-Reading Skills Early Numeracy Creative Arts
📘

Primary Textbooks

Our flagship product line covers all core subjects for Basic 1 through Basic 6. Each title is meticulously aligned with the Nigerian national curriculum and developed by experienced subject specialists. Subjects include English Language, Mathematics, Basic Science, Social Studies, Civic Education, Cultural & Creative Arts, and more.

Basic 1–6 All Core Subjects NLC Aligned Full Colour
📋

Supplementary Texts

Designed to extend and enrich classroom learning, our supplementary titles provide additional reading, comprehension practice, and enrichment activities beyond the core textbook. These materials are ideal for revision, holiday programmes, and gifted learner extension work.

Enrichment Revision Comprehension Extension
👩‍🏫

Teaching Guides

Our comprehensive teaching guides are developed to empower educators at every level. Each guide provides detailed lesson plans, learning objectives, suggested activities, differentiation strategies, and assessment ideas — enabling teachers to deliver engaging, effective, and curriculum-compliant lessons with confidence.

Lesson Plans Assessment Tools Differentiation Teacher Support
✏️

Workbooks

Our subject-specific workbooks provide structured, progressive exercises that reinforce core concepts and build subject mastery. Designed for use alongside the main textbooks, workbooks include formative assessments, practice problems, and activities that track and consolidate pupil progress throughout the school year.

Practice Exercises Self-Assessment Progressive Levels All Subjects
