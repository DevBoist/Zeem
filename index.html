Who We Are

A Trusted Name in Nigerian Educational Publishing

Founded in 2011, Zeem Global Publications Limited has grown into one of South-western Nigeria's most respected educational publishers. We develop carefully crafted, curriculum-aligned learning resources that speak directly to the realities of Nigerian classrooms.

With an in-house printing press and a team of experienced education professionals, we control every stage of production — ensuring consistent quality from manuscript to final delivery.
